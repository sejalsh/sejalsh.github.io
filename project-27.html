Fixy - iPhone App

Fixy is a professional handyman and cleaning service, which helps solve all those needs easily through an app. It covers almost all handyman services like carpentery, plumbing, office cleaning etc. with a list of exact problems for each category. You can schedule an appointment and get a realtime quote on adding different services to the cart. I collaborated with two of my friends to work on the UI, UX and visual design to make it a simple, beautiful and hassle-free app.
