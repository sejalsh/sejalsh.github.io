Intel - Pookalam Kaleidoscope

On the occasion of Onam, we created a FB app for Intel, letting users create visually stunning Pookalam using flowers and diyas. They can wish their friends a happy Onam by sharing their own unique Pookalam in the form of an e-card. This activity successfully brought together Indian culture and Intel tecnology for a visually stunning performance.
