Mahindra Thar - Brand Communication

Mahindra & Mahindra is one of the largest vehicle manufacturers by production in India. Mahindra being one of Interactive Avenues’ biggest client, I was part of the team handling Mahindra Thar, which is one of the most affordable 4x4 SUVs in India. The brand communication reflects the off-roader attitude of going places where other cars won’t dare go. Along with the strong typography, creating a visual tone was really important in bringing out the 'Thar Can Do' attitude.
