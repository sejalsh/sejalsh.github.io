Hello!

I am a UX designer at Trade Me, working on websites and apps for past 7 years. And this is what I do most of the time — Create something beautiful. Learn something new. And that is what makes me a happy designer! Other than reading, star gazing and collecting wind-up toys.

Design experience

Since my introduction to design, I have a strong inclination towards identifying / understanding the product goals clearly through research and creating beautiful, intuitive solutions for it. More than 7 years into design, I've been designing digital products and experiences that include researching, wireframing and prototyping. I've got opportunities to work on brands like — Mitre10, Intel, Diesel, Hamleys, Bloomberg, Castrol and on different products like — mobile apps, websites, digital magazine and more. It excites me to experiment and play around the new tools and processes emerging in the design industry.
