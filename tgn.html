The Great Next

The Great Next is an adventure travel startup, where you can search and book a wide range of adventure trips all over the world. Travel is an experience; its always about that one or various moments of peace between the starting point and destination. That moment is what we've tried to bring forward with the TGN logo. The logo has variations reflecting the different destinations and moods of the trip. The edgy style of the logo is continued in the iconography. It was great fun creating the icons due to the constraints of the shape. I also worked on the UI and visual design of the online booking portal, where people can find and book trips based on their choice of activity, difficulty level and other factors.
